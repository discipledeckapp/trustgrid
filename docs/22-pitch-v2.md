## Slide 4: The Solution

## TrustGrid

### Community Trust Infrastructure

TrustGrid gives communities the infrastructure to answer:

*"Who can be trusted here, and why?"*

Through five core capabilities:

**1. Community Hierarchy Engine**
Model any organizational structure — RCCG's 8-level hierarchy, a single estate, a university department. Unlimited depth. Configurable node types.

**2. Authority Engine**
Trust without authority is noise. TrustGrid assigns verifiable roles — Parish Pastor, Area Coordinator, Estate Manager — with explicit permissions and audit trails.

**3. Trust Passport**
Every person and organization receives a digital Trust Passport with credentials, endorsements, and a unique QR code for real-time verification.

**4. Endorsement Engine**
Endorsements are only valuable when backed by verified authority. Every endorsement in TrustGrid traces to its issuing authority.

**5. Opportunity Network**
Communities publish trust-gated opportunities — jobs, volunteer roles, event staffing, ministry positions. Only eligible, trusted members see them.
