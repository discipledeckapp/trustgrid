# TrustGrid — Business Design

**Version:** 1.0  

---

## Business Model Philosophy

TrustGrid does not require institutions to create new budget lines.

Every institution already spends money on:
- Workforce management (manually)
- Vendor onboarding (manually)
- Event staffing (through agencies)
- Background verification (ad hoc, if at all)
- Incident resolution (reactively, expensively)

TrustGrid captures a portion of existing spend, eliminates waste, and delivers measurably better outcomes.

---

## Who Pays and Why

### Tier 1: Community & Estate Operators

**Profile:** Gated estate management companies, residential communities, HOAs

**Pain:** Managing 50–200 domestic workers, technicians, maintenance staff. No system, no records, incidents happen without history.

**What they pay for:** Workforce Governance Subscription — managing their trusted worker registry and vendor governance.

**Why they pay:** One incident with an unverified worker costs ₦100K–₦5M in liability, theft, or reputational damage. TrustGrid costs ₦20K–₦80K/month. The ROI is 10x.

**Contract structure:** Monthly or annual SaaS subscription per institution.

---

### Tier 2: Churches & Religious Organizations

**Profile:** RCCG, Winners Chapel, Daystar, CAC with >2,000 member congregations; run events, welfare programs, community services

**Pain:** Managing volunteers and temporary workers for events. No central registry. Same workers cannot be re-engaged because nobody tracked them.

**What they pay for:** Volunteer Registry + Convention Staffing Module

**Why they pay:** RCCG runs 50+ events per year. Each event wastes 20–40 hours of staff time on manual workforce coordination. At ₦5,000/hour fully-loaded staff cost, that is ₦500K–₦1M wasted per event, 50x per year = ₦25M–₦50M/year.

**Contract structure:** Annual subscription + per-event staffing module activation.

---

### Tier 3: Schools & Universities

**Profile:** Secondary schools, polytechnics, universities managing facility vendors and security contractors

**Pain:** Multiple vendors, no performance history, no procurement trail, compliance risk.

**What they pay for:** Vendor Registry + Procurement Governance Module

**Why they pay:** Procurement compliance requirements, donor/accreditation requirements for vendor governance.

**Contract structure:** Annual institutional license.

---

### Tier 4: Facility Management Companies

**Profile:** Companies managing 5–50 properties (Primrose Estate, VGC Management, etc.)

**Pain:** Managing workforce across multiple sites. Worker at Site A performs poorly, Site B hires the same worker with no knowledge.

**What they pay for:** Multi-site Workforce Governance Subscription

**Why they pay:** Unified workforce visibility across all sites. Cross-site reputation carries over.

**Contract structure:** Per-property or flat-rate multi-property subscription.

---

### Tier 5: Convention & Event Organizers

**Profile:** Kingdom Business Network, RCCG Convention, Shiloh, Covenant University events

**Pain:** Staffing 200–2,000 temporary workers for 3–7 day events. Agencies are expensive, unaccountable, and deliver unverified workers.

**What they pay for:** Convention Workforce Module — high-touch event staffing, workforce assignment, real-time tracking

**Why they pay:** Replacing a ₦5M agency spend with a ₦500K TrustGrid engagement that is more reliable and creates a permanent worker registry for future events.

**Contract structure:** Per-event licensing with optional annual subscription.

---

### Tier 6: Workers & Vendors (Supply Side)

**Profile:** Individual electricians, plumbers, cleaners, security personnel, vendors

**What they pay for:** Verification & Trust Certification — paid badge/profile that opens doors to institutions

**Why they pay:** Verified workers command 20–40% higher rates and receive more consistent work. Verification is a career investment.

**Pricing:** ₦5,000–₦25,000 one-time verification + optional ₦2,000/month premium profile

- Redemption City is being built now — we are infrastructure, not a feature

## Pricing Psychology

1. **Anchor to existing spend:** "Estate managers currently pay ₦50K–₦200K for manual worker screening and agency fees. TrustGrid replaces all of that for ₦45K/month."

2. **Risk narrative:** "One security incident with an unverified worker can cost ₦500K in legal fees. TrustGrid is ₦180K/year."

3. **Status signal:** "TrustGrid Verified" badge communicates safety to residents — estates use it as a differentiator in the estate market.

4. **Network effect pricing:** Workers want to be on the platform because institutions require it. Institutions get better workers because more are on the platform.
