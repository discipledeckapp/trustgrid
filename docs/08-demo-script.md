## Opening Hook (60 seconds)

> "Last year, at a major national convention in Nigeria, an electrical fire broke out backstage. The cause? A temporary worker with no formal verification, hired through a WhatsApp recommendation, had incorrectly wired the stage power system. Nobody knew he had a prior incident at another event — because that information lived in someone else's phone."

> "This is not a unique story. Every estate, every church, every school, every event organizer in Nigeria has a version of this story. Workers are hired by trust, performance is managed by memory, and when things go wrong, there is no institutional record."

> "TrustGrid ends that. We are the trust operating system for communities, institutions, and smart cities."

## Part 4: Convention Staffing — The Core Demo (3 minutes)

**Click:** Service Requests → Create New Request

> "It's May 2026. The Convention Operations team needs to staff the annual convention."

**Fill in the form:**
- Title: "Convention Electricians — May 2026"
- Category: Electrical
- Workers Needed: 50
- Minimum Trust Score: **65**
- Dates: May 15–18, 2026
- Location: Redemption Camp

> "Minimum trust score: 65. This is governance. Not 'send anyone'. Send verified, trusted people."

**Click Submit → View Matched Workers**

> "TrustGrid instantly finds 67 workers in the registry who qualify. Verified identity, trust score above 65, Electrician skill, available those dates."

**Show the filtered list:**

> "Ranked by trust score. I can see at a glance who the best workers are. Let me select the top 50."

**Select workers → Click Assign**

> "50 workers assigned. Each one receives a notification — their assignment details, location, dates, supervisor contact."

**Switch to mobile phone — worker app:**

> "On Chukwuemeka's phone — he just got his assignment. He can see who hired him, where to report, and for how long. He taps 'Accept'."

**Switch back to dashboard:**

> "Assignment accepted. The dashboard updates in real time. The Operations Manager now has a staffed, verified, accountable workforce — in under 2 minutes."

## Q&A Preparation

**Q: How is this different from just using WhatsApp?**
> "WhatsApp is ephemeral. The moment a contact leaves, the trust data is gone. TrustGrid creates permanent institutional memory. The estate you manage today builds a trust registry that your successor inherits tomorrow."

**Q: Won't workers fake the NIN verification?**
> "NIN verification is done against NIMC's database with face match. The NIN is tied to a face. But beyond identity, the trust score is built from real deployments — you can't fake 23 completed jobs and 47 employer reviews."

**Q: Why won't a big tech company just build this?**
> "Because this requires deep institutional relationships and trust. TrustGrid is distribution-first. We start inside the RCCG ecosystem — 5 million members, 800+ churches. By the time a tech company notices the market, TrustGrid owns the institutional layer."

**Q: What's the business model?**
> "Institutions pay a monthly subscription. Workers pay for verification. Events pay per engagement. Year 1 target: 50 institutions, ₦47M revenue."

**Q: Is this Nigeria-only?**
> "Nigeria first. Architecture built for Ghana, Kenya, Rwanda, South Africa. The identity adapter system means we plug in each country's identity infrastructure without rewriting the core."
