# TrustGrid — Revenue Model

**Version:** 1.0  

---

## Core Principle: Capture Existing Spend

TrustGrid does not ask institutions to create new budgets. Every revenue stream targets money already being spent on:

- Manual verification (security companies, background check services)
- Agency workforce fees (staffing agencies for events and estates)
- Incident resolution (legal fees, compensation, management time)
- Procurement management (staff time on vendor coordination)
- Workforce coordination (admin staff time on manual processes)

**We replace those costs with something better, at a lower total cost.**

## Revenue Stream 2: Convention & Event Module

**Who pays:** RCCG, event organizers, convention operators, large church events  
**Why they pay:** Staff 50–2,000 verified temporary workers for a 3–7 day event  
**Existing spend being replaced:** Staffing agency fees (typically 15–25% markup on worker rates)  

### Pricing

| Event Scale | Workers | Price |
|-------------|---------|-------|
| Small Event | 50–100 workers | ₦150,000 flat |
| Medium Event | 100–500 workers | ₦400,000 flat |
| Large Event | 500–2,000 workers | ₦1,200,000 flat |
| National Convention | 2,000+ workers | Custom (₦2M–₦5M) |

**Pricing rationale:**  
A staffing agency for 500 workers charges 15% markup on ₦5,000/day/worker over 3 days = ₦1,125,000 in agency fees.  
TrustGrid charges ₦400,000 for a better outcome — pre-verified workers with deployment history and post-event trust score updates.  
**Savings for organizer: ₦725,000. Value-add: permanent registry that improves with each event.**

**RCCG-specific opportunity:**  
Conservative estimate: RCCG runs 4 major events per year requiring 500+ workers.  
Annual revenue potential from RCCG alone: 4 × ₦400,000–₦1,200,000 = **₦1.6M–₦4.8M/year**

## Revenue Stream 3: Verification Services

**Who pays:** Workers and vendors (demand-side)  
**Why they pay:** Being TrustGrid Verified opens access to institutions that mandate it, commanding 20–40% higher rates  
**Existing spend being replaced:** Ad-hoc background check services (₦5K–₦30K per check)  

### Pricing

| Level | What's Included | Worker Price | Institutional Bulk Price |
|-------|----------------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Basic | Document upload + NIN match | ₦5,000 | ₦3,500 |
| Standard | NIN + BVN + face match | ₦12,000 | ₦8,000 |
| Professional | Standard + credential verification | ₦25,000 | ₦18,000 |
| Enterprise | Professional + background check | ₦50,000 | ₦35,000 |

### Verification Revenue Model

Two channels:
1. **Worker self-pays:** Worker pays to get verified to access more work
2. **Institution bulk-pays:** Institution pays to verify their entire workforce

When an institution onboards 100 workers:
- They pay ₦8,000/worker (Standard bulk rate) = ₦800,000 one-time
- TrustGrid processes verifications at ~₦3,000 cost per worker (API calls + processing)
- Gross margin: ~62%

Annual verification volume estimate (Year 1): 2,000 verifications
Annual verification revenue: ₦2,000 × ₦10,000 avg = **₦20,000,000**

## Customer Acquisition

### Channel 1: Kingdom Hack 3.0 (Immediate)

- Direct access to RCCG leadership and institutional decision-makers
- Goal: 3 pilot commitments from the hackathon floor
- Conversion: Demo → Pilot Agreement → Paid Subscription

### Channel 2: RCCG Institutional Mandate (Month 1–3)

- Secure endorsement from RCCG Convention Committee
- Present to RCCG Heads of Department (HOD) conference
- Goal: Mandate TrustGrid as the standard for all RCCG events
- Conversion: 1 mandate → 50 churches in Year 1

### Channel 3: Estate Manager Networks (Month 2–6)

- Partner with 2–3 Lagos facility management companies
- Offer free 3-month pilot for first 10 estates
- ROI case study → referral network
- Goal: 20 estates in Year 1

### Channel 4: School & University Partnerships (Month 6–12)

- Partner with accreditation body (NUC for universities)
- Align vendor governance with compliance requirements
- Goal: 10 educational institutions in Year 1

### Channel 5: Direct Sales (Ongoing)

- 2 full-time sales reps in Year 1
- Target: each rep closes 2 institutions/month
- Fully-loaded cost per rep: ₦3M/year
- Revenue generated per rep (24 institutions × ₦45K avg MRR): ₦12.96M/year ARR
- Sales efficiency: 4:1 ARR:sales cost
